Privacy Policy for Giant Voice Stopwatch

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Giant Voice Stopwatch is a stopwatch application developed by Milan Opačić. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

Information the App Uses

Microphone

The app may request microphone permission when you enable voice control. The microphone is used only to recognize commands such as Start, Stop, Lap, and Reset. The app does not record, save, or upload audio recordings.

Voice recognition may be provided by your device operating system or its speech recognition service. Those services may process audio according to their own privacy policies.

Music files

You may optionally select an audio file stored on your device for local music playback. The selected file is used only on your device and is not uploaded, shared, or stored on our servers.

Stopwatch and lap data

Stopwatch values and lap times are used locally while the app is running. The app does not require an account and does not operate a server that stores your stopwatch data or voice recordings.

Advertising

Giant Voice Stopwatch displays ads through Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). Google and its advertising partners may collect and process certain device, advertising, diagnostic, and usage information to provide, measure, and improve advertising.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information processed by advertising or device speech-recognition services is handled by those providers under their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Giant Voice Stopwatch is not designed specifically for children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

Security

The app is designed to keep stopwatch activity and selected music files on your device whenever possible. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app or its data practices change. The latest version will always be available at this page.
